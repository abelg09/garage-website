Build a services section inspired by honza-digital.framer.website.

Use Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, and Framer Motion.

- Dark premium background with subtle vertical grid lines
- Left side sticky content:
  - Small label: [ MY SERVICES ]
  - Heading: What I Can Help You
  - Paragraph: I help businesses grow their online presence and generate real results through data-driven marketing strategies.
  - CTA button: Get in Touch

Animation requirement:
Create a sticky stacking card animation on the right side.
The left content should stay sticky while the user scrolls through the service cards.
Each service card should become sticky at the top of the right column.
As the user scrolls, the next card should move up and overlap the previous card.
Cards should feel like they are stacking one above another.
Add smooth scale and opacity animation using Framer Motion.
The active card should be full size.
Previous cards should slightly scale down and move upward.
Use scroll progress to animate each card.
